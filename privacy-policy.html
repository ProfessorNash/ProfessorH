🔒 Privacy Policy for Instant Audit

Last updated: June 15, 2025

Instant Audit (the "App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains that the App does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

📱 No Data Collection

The App does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All information you enter (salary calculations, expenses, notes, and income records) is stored only on your device using your browser’s built-in localStorage.

🔐 No Third‑Party Services

Instant Audit does not use any analytics trackers, crash reporters, advertising networks, or any third‑party services. The App functions entirely offline.

🗂️ Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Using the "Clear All" buttons inside the App (e.g., clear notes, clear history, clear daily income).
  • Uninstalling the App – this will remove all stored data from your device.

👶 Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13, and because no data is collected, no special restrictions apply.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of any revised policy.
