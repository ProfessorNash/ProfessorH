100% Offline & Private by Design
Instant Audit was built with a simple principle: your financial data belongs to you and only you. The app does not connect to the internet, collects no personal data, and stores everything exclusively on your own device.
01

Introduction

Welcome to Instant Audit, a financial dashboard app designed for Filipino small business owners, freelancers, and solo entrepreneurs. This Privacy Policy explains how Instant Audit handles your information.

We believe privacy is a right, not a feature — and this app was built from the ground up to reflect that commitment.

02

Information We Collect

Instant Audit collects absolutely no personal data. All information you enter — payroll figures, sales data, expenses, order notes, and income records — stays on your device only.

✕ No account or login required
✕ No names or emails collected
✕ No location data accessed
✕ No financial data transmitted
✕ No usage analytics or telemetry
✕ No crash reports sent externally
✕ No advertising identifiers
✕ No third-party trackers
03

How Your Data Is Stored

All app data — including payroll entries, income statements, sticky notes, calendar history, and settings — is saved locally on your device using standard Android WebView local storage.

This means your data is private by design and accessible only by you on your device. There is no cloud backup, no sync, and no remote access of any kind. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data.

04

Third-Party Services

Instant Audit does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

The app uses html2canvas — an open-source JavaScript library — locally and entirely offline to generate receipt images. This library runs only on your device and does not transmit any data externally.

05

Internet Access

Instant Audit is a fully offline application. No internet connection is required to use any of its features. The app does not make network requests and does not communicate with any external servers.

07

Children's Privacy

Instant Audit is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since no data is collected from any user at all, there is no risk of inadvertent collection from minors.

08

Data Security

Because all data remains on your device and is never transmitted, the security of your data depends on the security of your own device. We recommend the following best practices:

  • 1 Use a screen lock, PIN, or biometric lock on your device.
  • 2 Keep your Android OS and security patches up to date.
  • 3 Back up your device regularly if you wish to preserve your app data.
09

Data Deletion

You are in full control of your data. To delete all data stored by Instant Audit:

  • 1 Open the app and go to Settings.
  • 2 Tap "Clear All Notes Data" to remove all sticky notes and order entries.
  • 3 Tap "Clear All Daily Income" to remove all calendar income entries.
  • 4 Alternatively, uninstalling the app from your device will permanently remove all locally stored data.
